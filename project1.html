Dissertation: Assessing the Impact of Corruption on Economic Growth

Project Overview

This dissertation involved a cross-country analysis of how corruption impacts economic growth. The core of the project was to apply international datasets, evaluate existing policies, and use advanced data modelling to find statistical relationships. [cite: 11]

Key Skills & Methods

  • Statistical Modelling: Applied statistical models using Excel and Python to analyze the data.
  • Data Integrity: Conducted reconciliations and consistency checks, similar to the process of a financial audit, to ensure data accuracy.
  • Policy Analysis: Produced policy-driven insights focused on global economic development and governance. [cite: 27]
